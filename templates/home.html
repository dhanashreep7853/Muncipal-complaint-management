How It Works

Filing and tracking your civic complaint takes less than 2 minutes. Our system ensures your voice reaches the right authority.

📝
01
Register & Verify

Create your account with email OTP verification — ensures only genuine citizens file complaints.

📸
02
Submit with Evidence

Describe your complaint, upload a photo, and share your GPS location for precise identification.

🏛️
03
Auto-Routed

Your complaint is instantly routed to the correct state municipal authority for action.

📊
04
Track in Real Time

Use your Complaint ID to check status and see the resolution deadline at any time.
